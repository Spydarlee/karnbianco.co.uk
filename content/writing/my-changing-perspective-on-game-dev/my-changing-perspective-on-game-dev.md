### Are You Feeling Lucky?

I want to start by emphasizing just how lucky I was with my initial break into the industry, and the many other opportunities I've had since. Well, they're probably just lazy and didn't want it enough in the first place.

Poppycock! The world of games was always a world built for me: the young, geeky, straight, white, cisgender male who grew up playing games. ### The Limits (and Exploitation) of Passion

Overwork is often driven by the developers themselves, not just some overbearing management team, but that doesn't make it any less damaging. It can be hard to recognise how much is too much when you're enjoying yourself, but we all have limits and it will catch up with you eventually (take it from me). Also, the desire for a healthy work-life balance doesn't mean you're less passionate than your peers or any less deserving of your place among them.
